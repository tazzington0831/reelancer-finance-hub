How to Build an Emergency Fund with Irregular Income

An emergency fund is your safety net when freelance income fluctuates. Learn how to build one even with unpredictable cash flow.

  1. Calculate your essential monthly expenses.
  2. Set a realistic savings target (3–6 months of expenses).
  3. Open a separate high‑yield savings account.
  4. Automate a percentage of each payment into your emergency fund.
  5. Use windfalls to boost your savings when possible.
