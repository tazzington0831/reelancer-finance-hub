Top 5 Budgeting Apps for Freelancers

Staying on top of your finances as a freelancer can be tough. Here are five budgeting apps that simplify tracking income and expenses.

  • YNAB: Uses zero‑based budgeting to assign every dollar a job.
  • Mint: Aggregates your accounts and automatically categorizes transactions.
  • PocketGuard: Shows how much money is “in your pocket” after bills and savings.
  • EveryDollar: Streamlined interface with a zero‑based approach.
  • Goodbudget: Digital envelope budgeting for partners and families.
